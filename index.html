The Process

How AI4Change Works

Like GitHub Issues, but for the world's problems. Like Playing for Change, but AI is the instrument.

01
🗣️

Post a Problem

Anyone, anywhere can submit a real-world problem they face — flooding, crop disease, lack of translated textbooks, anything.

02
📊

Community Signals

People upvote, discuss, and refine problems. AI bots aggregate sentiment across platforms. The most urgent issues rise to the top.

03
🔥

Trending Board

A public, transparent dashboard shows what the world wants solved right now. When a problem hits critical mass — we commit.

04
🔨

Build Together

Contributors from anywhere join the build. Each uses AI as their multiplier — code, data, design, local knowledge. All welcome.

05
📦

Ship Open Source

The solution goes live. Free. Open. Documented. A building block for the next project.

06
🎬

Tell the Story

A beautiful video showing the humans behind the solution — filmed across continents, stitched together like a Song Around the World.
