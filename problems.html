How It Works

From Problem to Solution

Three steps from a real-world challenge to an open-source solution built by the community.

📝
Step 01

Submit

Anyone can submit a real-world problem their community faces. No code required, just a clear description of the challenge.

📣
Step 02

Community Signals

The community upvotes, discusses, and refines problems. Champions emerge to lead the solution effort.

🚀
Step 03

Build Together

Teams form around high-signal problems. They build open-source AI solutions that anyone can use and adapt.
