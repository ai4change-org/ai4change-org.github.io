Your Path

How to Contribute

From first click to shipped solution. Here's the journey every contributor takes.

1
Step 1

Explore

Browse the Problems Board. Find something that resonates. Read the discussions. Understand the context and the people behind each problem.

2
Step 2

Signal

React, comment, or upvote on problems you care about. Your signal helps prioritize what the community works on next. Every voice counts.

3
Step 3

Claim

See a problem you want to champion? Comment "I'll take this" and we'll help you get started. No gatekeeping, no permission needed.

4
Step 4

Build

Fork, branch, build. Use AI as your co-pilot. Collaborate with others in the open. Submit pull requests when you're ready.

5
Step 5

Ship

Your solution gets reviewed, merged, and documented. Your story becomes part of the movement. The world gets a little better.
