The Candidates

Three Problems. One First Song.

These three seed problems rose to the top. Each one could change millions of lives. Which one should the community tackle first?

1
Environment

Water Quality Monitoring

2 billion people drink contaminated water daily. They know it might be bad. They drink it anyway, because there is no alternative and no warning system.

💧
AI-powered early warning Sensors + satellite imagery detecting contamination in real time, alerting communities before people get sick.
Vote on GitHub
2
Agriculture

Crop Disease Detection

Smallholder farmers lose up to 40% of their harvest to diseases they can't identify until it's too late. Entire seasons. Entire livelihoods. Gone.

🌱
Phone-camera AI diagnosis Point your phone at a sick plant, get an instant diagnosis and treatment plan. The models exist in research papers — we put them in farmers' hands.
Vote on GitHub
3
Education

Education Content Generation

250 million children lack access to quality learning materials in their own language. Outdated textbooks written for a different culture — or no textbooks at all.

📚
AI-generated local curriculum A math lesson in Quechua. Science in Amharic. Locally relevant, pedagogically sound, infinitely reproducible — and free.
Vote on GitHub
Transparent & Open

How Voting Works

No hidden algorithms. No backroom deals. Just GitHub reactions.

1

Go to the Issue

Each problem is a GitHub Issue in our problems repo. Click "Vote on GitHub" on any card above.

2

React with 👍

Add a thumbs-up reaction to the issue you want the community to build first. One reaction per person.

3

Watch It Happen

The problem with the most reactions becomes our first song. Then we find a champion and start building.
